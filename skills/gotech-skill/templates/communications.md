## Pain Points

1. **Inconsistent production quality** — Content looks amateur vs. competitors, no brand consistency
2. **Multi-platform distribution nightmare** — Each platform needs different formats, aspect ratios, captions; posting manually
3. **Time drain** — Content production takes 3x longer than it should (editing, formatting, scheduling)
4. **No repurposing system** — One piece of content gets used once instead of across 5+ channels
5. **Scaling audience without scaling team** — Growing following but can't keep up with production demands

## Value Proposition

**Full Communications Stack — Professional content production at startup speed.**

We handle your entire communications pipeline: content strategy, production (video, audio, written), multi-platform formatting, distribution, and analytics. You get a full communications department's output without the headcount.

**What you get:**
- Content strategy & editorial calendar
- Professional video/audio production & editing
- Multi-platform formatting (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X, Podcasts)
- Distribution automation & scheduling
- Monthly performance reporting

## Objection Handling

### 1. "Too expensive / We don't have budget"

"Completely understand. Most of our clients were spending $5K–$8K/month on freelance editors and designers before us — we typically come in at the same price with way more output. Would it help to start with a single production workflow (e.g., podcast editing + distribution) and expand from there?"

### 2. "Now is not the right time"

"Totally fair. Most teams hit a point where they can't ignore it anymore — usually when they're losing audience growth or burning out the team. What would need to change for this to become a priority?"

### 3. "We need someone in-house"

"You could hire — but a good video editor + distribution manager + strategist runs $120K–$180K in salary alone. We deliver the same stack at a fraction of that, and you can redirect to part-time contractors for specific needs. Want me to show you the math?"

### 4. "We already have tools for this"

"You probably do — most creators and agencies have 5–8 tools that don't talk to each other. We specialize in connecting your existing tools into one pipeline. Want me to map out your current stack and show you where the gaps are?"

### 5. "I need to talk to my team/pastor/partners"

"Absolutely. I can put together a 1-pager or 5-min walkthrough video your team can review together. Sometimes it helps to have the specifics laid out when having that internal conversation."
