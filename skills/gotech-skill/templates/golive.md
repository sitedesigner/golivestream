## Target Persona

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Titles** | Tech Director, Media Director, Worship Leader, Content Director, Founder, Event Manager |
| **Company Size** | 5–50 employees (production/event companies), 20–1000+ (churches), Solo (influencers/educators) |

| **Decision Maker** | Tech/media director, pastor/leadership team, or founder |

## Pain Points

1. **Complex technical setup** — Streaming requires cameras, switchers, encoding, audio mixing; steep learning curve
2. **Reaching wider audience** — Current audience limited to in-person attendees; massive untapped online reach
3. **Production quality gap** — Can't match professional streamers; equipment is expensive and underutilized
4. **Multi-platform streaming** — Want to stream to YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, custom site simultaneously
5. **Reliability concerns** — Fear of going live and technical failure; no redundancy or backup plan

## Value Proposition

**Go Live Stream — Professional streaming without the production headache.**

We handle everything: technical setup, multi-platform streaming, production quality, redundancy systems, and ongoing support. You focus on your message and audience — we make it look and sound professional while reaching everyone, everywhere.

**What you get:**
- End-to-end streaming technical setup (cameras, switcher, encoding, audio)
- Multi-platform simultaneous streaming (YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, custom)
- Professional production quality (graphics, overlays, lower-thirds)
- Redundancy & failover systems (no more dead air)
- On-call support for every live event

## Objection Handling

### 1. "Too expensive / We don't have budget"

"Understand completely. Here's the thing though: you're already investing in streaming equipment, and one-time production cost is roughly equivalent to 3 months of in-house. Most [churches/influencers] actually save money by not needing to hire a dedicated tech person."

### 2. "Now is not the right time"

"Totally fair. Most teams start thinking about streaming when they're frustrated with their current setup or they're missing a big opportunity. What would need to change for this to become a priority?"

### 3. "Our team can handle it"

"Absolutely — most can. The question is: are they handling it well, or just managing it and actually delivering streams with proper quality and consistency? We've found that even when teams can do it themselves, they are usually stretched thin — and streams suffer. We can either take full ownership, or supplement your team for a few weeks while we fine-tune the setup."

### 4. "We don't have the right equipment"

"We work with a wide range of equipment budgets — from $5K to $50K setups. For most teams, I'd recommend starting with a solid foundation ($8K–$15K) that reliably supports your streams and can grow with you. Want me to send over sample gear lists at different budget levels?"

### 5. "We need leadership approval"

"Of course. Want me to put together a 1-pager or 5-min video your leadership team can review? tend to speed up internal alignment when they can see the specifics. No pressure."
