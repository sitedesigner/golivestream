## Pain Points

1. **Manual processes** — Onboarding, customer support, and internal workflows still done by hand, eating 20+ hrs/week
2. **Slow customer support** — Response times hurting NPS and retention, no automation in place
3. **Content creation bottlenecks** — Marketing and documentation always behind schedule, can't scale output
4. **Tool sprawl** — 15+ SaaS tools with no integration, data silos everywhere
5. **Hiring pressure** — Can't hire fast enough, but current team can't handle more workload

## Value Proposition

**AI Operations Automation — Handle 10x the work without hiring.**

We integrate AI into your existing stack to automate repetitive operations: customer support responses, onboarding flows, content generation, internal reporting, and cross-tool workflows. Most clients save 15–20 hours per week immediately — that's half an FTE recovered, zero hires needed.

**What you get:**
- Automated customer support triage & response (24/7)
- AI-powered content generation pipeline (blog, social, docs)
- Auto-reporting dashboards across your entire stack
- Custom workflow automations connecting your tools
- Dedicated ops automation engineer (included)

## Objection Handling

### 1. "Too expensive / We don't have budget"

"Totally get it. Our average client sees $3,600–$6,000/month in recovered staff time — most recover our fee in the first 3–4 weeks. Happy to start with a pilot on one workflow so you can see the ROI before committing to the full stack. What's your current cost of a wasted hour?"

### 2. "Now is not the right time"

"Understood — timing matters. Most of us waited until the pain was unbearable (team burnout, missed deadlines). What would need to change for this to become a priority? I can have something ready to go when you are."

### 3. "We need to build this in-house"

"You absolutely could — and I'd expect 3–6 months of one senior engineer's time to match what we deliver in 2 weeks. Is your ops team available to spend the next quarter building internal tooling? Usually the answer is no, and that's exactly why we exist."

### 4. "Not sure it would work with our stack"

"We work with 40+ common tools (Slack, Notion, Zendesk, Linear, HubSpot, etc.) and build custom integrations for anything else. Want to send me your top 5 tools? I'll confirm compatibility within 24 hours — no commitment."

### 5. "I need to talk to my co-founder / team"

"Absolutely — want me to put together a 1-pager or 5-min video walkthrough your team can review internally? Makes the internal conversation easier when they can see the specifics."
