## Objection Handling

### 1. "Too still expensive even at 50% off"

"This is actually half-price — we specifically designed it to be the lowest possible entry point, and it's only for the first 20 people. Wait — that's what I said. Here's another way: over 3 months you're paying [amount], and if you just get one additional client/revenue event, it's been worthwhile. And you can cancel after month 3 if it doesn't work — no strings attached beyond that."

### 2. "Now is not the right time"

"Totally get it — timing is always tricky. But this offer literally expires when spots fill. Most people overestimate how long "right timing" takes. 3 months is enough to see ROI, you can cancel after, and you'll never get this price again. Want to start at the minimum viable level and prove it over 90 days?"

### 3. "Need references / case studies first"

"That's exactly why we're doing the Founding Member Program — we're building those case studies right now with the first cohort. You'd be part of that group. We can also connect you with any of the [service] clients we've worked with (past ones) — that's a separate reference, not part of this program. Want me to set up a call with any of them?"

### 4. "Want to see more details first"

"Of course — what specific questions do you have? I'll get you a full breakdown. Spots are filling though, so I'd recommend quick yes/no on the pricing. Everything else we can figure out together — the offer is only 3 months, no commitment beyond that. What would help you decide?"

### 5. "Need to talk to my co-founder / partners"

"Absolutely — want me to put together a quick 1-pager that lays it out for them? Spots are going though, so ideally get their input fast. Shoot me a quick 'yes' and I'll send over a founder summary perfect for internal review. No pressure, just want to make sure your team can see this while it's available."
