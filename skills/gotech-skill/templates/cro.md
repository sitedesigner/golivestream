## Pain Points

1. **Low conversion rates** — Traffic is coming but not converting; leaky funnel
2. **Wasted ad spend** — Burning $10K+/mo on ads with diminishing returns
3. **Landing page underperformance** — Pages don't communicate value effectively; A/B testing is ad-hoc
4. **Cart/s Checkout abandonment** — 60–80% of carts abandoned with no recovery system
5. **No testing framework** — No systematic way to identify and fix conversion bottlenecks

## Value Proposition

We audit your entire conversion funnel, identify the highest-impact bottlenecks, and implement changes that typically yield 15–40% conversion rate improvements. No more guessing — data-driven optimization that pays for itself in recovered ad spend alone.

**What you get:**
- Full funnel audit & heatmap analysis
- Landing page optimization & A/B test implementation
- Checkout/conversion flow redesign
- Ad spend efficiency analysis & recommendations
- Weekly optimization sprints with measurable impact

## Objection Handling

### 1. "Too expensive / We don't have budget"

"I get it. But think of it this way: if you're spending $10K/mo on ads and your conversion rate is 2%, a 1% improvement means $10K/mo more revenue. Our CRO work typically pays for itself in the first 2–4 weeks from recovered conversions alone. Can I show you the math for your specific situation?"

### 2. "Now is not the right time"

"Fair enough. Most clients come to us when they're spending $5K+/mo on ads with diminishing returns — that's usually when it becomes urgent. What would need to change for this to become a priority?"

### 3. "We already do A/B testing"

"That's great — most don't. The question is: are you testing the *right* things? Most teams test button colors when the real wins come from checkout flow, page speed, form design, and trust signals. Want me to share 3 quick wins we typically see that most teams miss?"

### 4. "We need more traffic, not CRO"

"More traffic is great — but if your funnel is leaking 70% of potential conversions, you're just pouring water into a sieve. Fix the funnel first, then scale traffic. You'll get 2–3x more revenue dollar you spend. Want to see math looks like?"

### 5. "I need to talk to my team"

"Absolutely. Want me to put together a 1-pager with 3 specific things we'd look at in your funnel? Makes it easy to discuss internally and usually gets people aligned faster."
