## 7. Pricing Strategy for Speed

### Founding Member Deal (First 20 Clients Only)
- **50% off first 3 months**
- Go Live: $200/mo (months 1–3), then $400/mo
- Comms: $150/mo (months 1–3), then $300/mo
- AI Ops: $250/mo (months 1–3), then $500/mo
- CRO: $375/mo (months 1–3), then $750/mo
- Bundle: $875/mo (months 1–3), then $1,750/mo
- **Savings to client:** $600–$2,625 over 3 months
- **Psychology:** Scarcity + exclusivity + urgency

### Annual Plan Incentive
- **Pay for 10 months, get 12** (2 months free = 17% discount)
- Cash upfront = runway extension
- Example: Bundle annual = $17,500 upfront vs $21,000 retail

### Bundle Bonus
- **Any 2 services = 20% off**
- Example: Go Live ($400) + AI Ops ($500) = $900 → $720/mo

### Referral Bonus
- **Refer a friend → both get 1 month free**
- Cost to business: $400–$1,750 (one month of service)
- Value acquired: $4,800–$21,000 annual client
- **ROI: 12x–52x**

### Flash Sale (48-Hour Urgency)
- **25% off first month** for any new signup within 48 hours
- Deployed in Phase 4 to accelerate pipeline
- Limited to 10 spots per flash

## 11. Risk Mitigation

### Scenario A: Conversion is Half of Target (0.18% instead of 0.36%)

| Impact | Mitigation |
|--------|-----------|
| 100 clients instead of 200 | Increase outreach to 800/day OR extend timeline to 90 days |
| $500K ARR instead of $1M | Focus on higher-ticket services (Bundle + CRO only) |
| Cash shortfall | Reduce burn, offer more annual plans for upfront cash |

**Action plan:** If Day 14 shows <15 clients, immediately:
1. Double outreach volume
2. Lower entry price to $297/mo for first month
3. Offer free 14-day trial to reduce friction
4. Pivot to outbound calling (phone > email)

### Scenario B: Average Deal Size is Lower ($300/mo instead of $572/mo)

| Impact | Mitigation |
|--------|-----------|
| Need 278 clients instead of 200 | Focus on volume: Go Live + Comms only |
| Lower revenue per client | Aggressively upsell to Bundle within 30 days |
| Slower cash flow | Offer 6-month prepay discount instead of annual |

**Action plan:** If average ticket <$350 by Day 30:
1. Bundle minimum: Go Live + Comms = $560/mo (20% off $700)
2. Require 3-month minimum commitment
3. Add onboarding fee ($500) to increase initial revenue

### Scenario C: 5% Monthly Churn

| Month | Starting Clients | Churn (5%) | New Needed | Net Clients |
|-------|-----------------|------------|------------|-------------|
| July | 30 | 1.5 | 15 | ~43 |
| August | 100 | 5 | 50 | ~145 |
| September | 170 | 8.5 | 40 | ~207 |

**Net result:** Need to acquire 230+ clients to maintain 200 active  
**Mitigation:**
1. Onboarding call in first 48 hours (reduce early churn)
2. Monthly value review calls (catch dissatisfaction early)
3. Annual plans = near-zero churn (locked in)
4. SLA guarantees + priority support for Bundle clients

| Trigger | Action |

| Cash < $200 | Freelance/consult to generate emergency revenue |

**Emergency fundraising options:**
- 10 founding clients × $2,000 annual upfront = $20,000
- 5 Bundle clients × $10,000 annual = $50,000
