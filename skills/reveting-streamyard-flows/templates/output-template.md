## Guest invite SOP

1. Create/open broadcast → click Invite → copy guest link
2. Send link via email (pre-show briefing template)
3. Pre-show tech check 15 min before go-live:
   - [ ] Guest opens link in Chrome or Firefox
   - [ ] Camera, microphone, internet confirmed
   - [ ] Guest name display verified
   - [ ] Brief on scene transitions
4. Backup: phone number for audio-only dial-in
5. Bring on screen: click guest tile → "Add to broadcast"
